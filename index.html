About

From a very young age, I was certain of one thing. I was born to tell stories. As, Lord Byron said, a drop of ink may make a million think, and I think I understood that long before I could put it into words.

As I grew up, I chose to turn that passion into something more. Something I could carry with me and share with the world.

But, this is not where I bore you with the professional details or a list of achievements. This is where the magic happens and I let my imagination take over.

If you are looking for tips and insights from what I have learned in the creative industry, you can explore my portfolio. If you are here to see what it feels like inside my mind, I would recommend the fiction section.

Because in the end, a writer is a world trapped inside a person, and this is mine.
