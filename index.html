About Me

A world
inside a
person

From a very young age, I was certain of one thing. I was born to tell stories. As Lord Byron said, a drop of ink may make a million think and I understood that long before I could put it into words.

As I grew up, I chose to turn that passion into something more. Something I could carry with me and share with the world.

But this is not where I bore you with professional details or a list of achievements. This is where the magic happens and I let my imagination take over.

If you're looking for insights from the creative industry, explore my portfolio. If you're here to see what it feels like inside my mind I'd recommend the fiction section.
